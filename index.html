About Us

Why are we WILD ?

It all started with Will,Ian,Lisa and Dan !

We first met on festivals as single performers, gradually became friends... and decided to perform together.

We started testing our performance together on festivals, and were happy to see people really enjoyed our perfomances.

We did this for a couple of years, and decided we wanted to go BIG.

By then other performers had joined us, and it was time for the WILD CIRCUS to put up it's first Circus Tent in 2004.

Since then we have kept on growing, and we are now 20 performers, touring all over Europe.

This is our Dream come true, and we hope to embark you with us in our (dream) WILD CIRCUS.
